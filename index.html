This pile costs UB, and you must have at least one card in hand other than Careful Study. This would be the default Pass-The-Turn pile. If you untap with UB, you can win off your draw for turn + blue looting. This is the natural follow up to Turn 1 Doomsday, but note that this sequence works anywhere in the stack.

Some cards to consider:

  • multiple Street Wraith
  • Narset, if you want to focus your piles to take advantage of blue devotion
  • Devastation Tide and Collective Brutality in the sideboard
  • Predict or Mental Note (à la Meandeck Doomsday) in place of Careful Study.
  • Dark Petition could slot in here
  • Leyline of Anticipation, Dark Petition, and Scheming Symmetry for an all-spell version
